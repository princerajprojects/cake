Cake is a beloved, sweet dessert traditionally made by baking a rich batter of flour, sugar, eggs, and fat. According to the Encyclopædia Britannica, historical baking methods evolved from ancient, honey-sweetened flatbreads into modern, fluffy structures raised with baking powder.
Today, this versatile confection ranges from light sponge cakes to dense, layered masterpieces. They are often covered in smooth buttercream frosting, vibrant fondants, or fresh fruits. Beyond being a delicious treat, cake serves as a global symbol of celebration, bringing joy to milestones like weddings, anniversaries, and birthday parties
